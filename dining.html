Dining Hall Menus

Main Hall Description

The Main Dining Hall is the heart of campus dining—large, lively, and always buzzing with activity. It offers the widest variety of daily meal options, including comfort foods, rotating chef specials, vegetarian/vegan selections, and a fresh salad bar. With plenty of seating and extended hours, it’s the go-to spot for students looking for a reliable, full-service meal experience.

North Hall Description

North Hall is perfect for students who want quick, customizable meals without sacrificing quality. Known for its build-your-own stations—like pasta bowls, burritos, stir-fry, and sandwiches—this dining hall is a favorite among commuters and students on a tight schedule. North Hall focuses on fast service, fresh ingredients, and grab-and-go convenience.

South Hall Description

South Hall offers a relaxed, cozy atmosphere with a more curated menu. It’s known for its themed cuisine nights, specialty dishes, and rotating international options. South Hall is a great choice for students looking to try something new or enjoy a quieter dining experience away from the busiest foot-traffic areas.
